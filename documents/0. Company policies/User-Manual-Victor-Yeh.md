  - there is mutual respect and open, transparent communication
  
- My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is...

  - requirements changing without prior notice or discussion
  - a lack of comments and comprehensive documentation
  
- If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means...
  - I do not have anything to contribute at that moment, but I am actively listening

- I stay focused by...

  - maintaining a structured task list
  - working in a quiet, distraction-free environment
  
- Things that distract me...
